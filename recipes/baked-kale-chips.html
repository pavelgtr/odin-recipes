Baked Kale Chips

Easy and yummy kale chips baked at home!

Ingredients

  • bunch kale
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon flaked sea salt

Directions

Gather Your Ingredients: Collect all the necessary ingredients.
  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven and prepare a rimmed baking sheet by lining it with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare the Kale: Carefully separate the kale leaves from the thick stems and tear them into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Wash and Dry the Kale: Thoroughly wash the kale and use a salad spinner to ensure it's completely dry.
  4. Coat with Olive Oil: Drizzle the kale leaves with olive oil and toss them to make sure they're well coated.
  5. Spread Out on Baking Sheet: Arrange the kale pieces in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet, ensuring they're not overlapping. Sprinkle some salt evenly over the kale.
  6. Bake to Perfection: Bake the kale until the edges start to turn brown, but be careful not to let them burn.
  7. Enjoy! Once they're done, take them out of the oven and savor your homemade baked kale chips.
